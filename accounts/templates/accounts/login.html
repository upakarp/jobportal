How It Works As Hirer | As Worker

Single account works as both hirer and worker account.

As Hirer

Your account works as an employer account. Post Jobs. Provide lob details. Specify Pay Amount and Workers Needed. Wait for the workers' bid. Based on reviews and work ratings, Hire your worker/s. At work completion, you pay your worker's zyaala.

As Worker

Your account also works as an job seeker. Setup Payment Transaction details. Maintain your Profile. Complete Work Permit Verification. Find Jobs Nearby. Bid your rates. Get Accepted and Hired. Start Working. Legwork or Online Works. Get Securely Paid.
